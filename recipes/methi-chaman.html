Methi Chaman

Description

Methi chaman is a delicious gravy based dish from the Kashmiri pandit cuisine. Methi is fenugreek leaves and chaman is paneer in Kashmiri language. The making of this iconic dish can be compared to the making of Palak Paneer.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp saunf (fennel seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp jeera (cumin seeds)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chilli, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1 cup fresh methi leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cashew paste (made from boiled cashews)
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • Salt to taste
  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ghee

Steps

  1. Prepare the Tadka
  2. Saute the onion mixture
  3. Add tomatoes and dry masalas
  4. Cook fresh methi
  5. Add cashew paste and simmer
  6. Finish with Paneer, Ghee and Cream
